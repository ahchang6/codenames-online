Now that I know how to play, how do I use this website?
To start, simply click on Create Room to generate a room with our list of words. The access code will then display, copy and distribute the access code to anyone who wants to join the room. Clicking join room with the access code will take you to the room where you can choose your team. Choosing your team then takes you to the game. The players will the wait for the players to choose the word for the corresponding team.

It seems cool, but I want to use my own words to play!
You can do that! On the main page, if you upload a text document with each word on a newline, our server will create a game with all your words in the game, and if there's more than 25, we will use your text document as the word bank instead of ours!
